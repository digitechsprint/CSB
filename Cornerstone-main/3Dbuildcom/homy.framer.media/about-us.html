FAQ

Things You Should Know

We’ve answered the most common questions to help you get started with clarity.

What makes CornerStone Buildcom different?

Investor-first approach combining 18+ years expertise, rigorous due diligence, integrated leasing, and comprehensive capital support beyond transactions.

Can I filter homes by budget & location?

Primary focus on Noida, Delhi NCR with strong capabilities across India's premium commercial and industrial corridors.

Do you support NRI investments?

Yes. Complete guidance on compliant structures, documentation, financing, and portfolio management for overseas investors.

How does your due diligence process work?

Comprehensive title verification, technical audits, compliance checks, site inspections, and financial evaluation ensuring risk-free decisions.

Can leasing improve investment returns?

Absolutely. Strategic leasing delivers higher ROI through faster occupancy, quality tenants, and long-term rental stability.

What funding solutions do you offer?

Commercial loans, LRD, working capital, industrial financing, PE/VC connections.

What's your typical client profile?

Serious investors, developers, corporates, retail brands, industrial players, startups, and NRIs.
